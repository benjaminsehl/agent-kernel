# Dreaming runbook

Inputs
- recent daily notes
- recently updated knowledge files
- recent filed outputs

Phase routine
1. Light: collect repeated signals and open loops.
2. REM: group them into themes and questions.
3. Deep: promote only stable, high-value conclusions.

Promotion rules
- Do not promote one-off events.
- Do not promote emotionally vivid but low-signal observations.
- Prefer under-writing to overfitting.
- If no meaningful promotion exists, say so.
